At its simplest level, geocaching requires these 8 steps:

  1. Register for a free Basic Membership.
  2. Visit the Find Em All! page.
  3. You can either enter your postal code and click "search", or enter the coordinates of the geocache
  4. Then your GPS device to assist you in finding the hidden geocache.
  5. Sign the Logbook and return the geocache to its original location.
  6. Repeat process and become a geocache master! Geocache Em' All!
